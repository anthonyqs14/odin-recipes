Causa

Recipes Homepage

Causa is one of Peru's most popular and exquisite dishes. Available at almost any Peruvian restaurant causa is sure to leave you wanting more after the first bite.

    Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Yukon Gold Potatoes,peeled
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1 tbsp aji amarillo paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 tbsp mayonaise
  • 2 tbsp red onion (finely diced)
  • 1 tsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley (minced)
  • Choice of stuffing (chicken,tuna,etc.)

Steps

  1. To make the potato layer, boil the potatoes in salted water until fork tender.
  2. Drain the potatoes, then pass them through a ricer. Cool the mashed potatoes to room temperature.
  3. Once the potatoes are cooled, add the olive oil, lime juice and aji amarillo paste. Stir to combine, then season the potato mixture with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. To make the causa rellena, line a casserole dish with plastic wrap.
  5. Fill the dish 1/3 of the way up with the potato mixture.
  6. Add a layer of sliced avocado, then a layer of tuna salad.
  7. Finally, top the casserole dish with another layer of potato.
  8. Wrap the causa rellena with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  9. To unmold the causa rellena, flip the causa onto a plate and remove the plastic wrap. Serve cold.
